RipReport — Terms of Use & Privacy

Last updated: July 2026

Prices are estimates, not offers

All card and sealed-product values shown in RipReport are informational estimates derived from third-party market data (including TCGplayer market prices and, where available, sold-comparable data). They are not appraisals, offers to buy or sell, or a guarantee of any sale price. RipReport is not a marketplace and does not broker, list, or facilitate sales of any kind. Profit & loss figures are computed from prices you enter and public market estimates, and may differ from what you would actually realize in a sale.

Your account and data

When you create an account we store your email address, a securely hashed password (never the password itself), and the collection data you choose to log — cards, quantities, the prices you paid, and sales you record. This data is used only to run the service: syncing your collection across devices and computing your portfolio figures. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

If you sign in with Google, we receive only your verified email address from Google; we never see your Google password.

Card scanning

When you use the scan feature, the photo you capture is sent to our server and forwarded to a third-party recognition service (such as Google Cloud Vision) solely to identify the card. Scan images are processed transiently and are not stored by RipReport.

Deleting your data

You can delete your account at any time from the Account screen. Deletion removes your account record, sessions, and synced collection data from our servers.

Fair use

Free accounts include a monthly allowance of card scans. Automated, abusive, or bulk access to the service is not permitted.

No warranty

RipReport is provided "as is", without warranties of any kind. Card names, images, and set names are the property of their respective owners; RipReport is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by The Pokémon Company, Nintendo, TCGplayer, or any card manufacturer or marketplace.

Contact

Questions about these terms or your data: contact the operator via the app's support channel.
